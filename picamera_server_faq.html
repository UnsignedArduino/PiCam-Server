Q: I can't see the stream! What do I do?
A: There are a couple options you could go for. You should proceed in the order listed.

  1. Refresh the page.
  2. Try both the mobile and desktop versions.
  3. Make sure that you actually showed the stream by pressing the button that says .
  4. Make sure your browser can support MJPEG streams. If you are using something like Internet Explorer, update to a more "modern" browser. I suggest Chrome.
  5. Closing and re-opening your browser.
  6. Restarting your device.
  7. Wait a couple minutes (or an hour). Sometimes, the server might be offline because of something as simple as a system update.
  8. Contact me, in person or by email at ckyiu@outlook.com . When contacting me, please be sure to include the time and date, what device it is (A computer/tablet/phone/etc.), what operating system version it is running (Click here or if you have Chrome, click here ), what browser version (On Chrome, click here to open your version page), and if you see any error codes (Like Error 404).
